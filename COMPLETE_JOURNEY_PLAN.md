# Homemade CEO — The Complete End-to-End Journey Plan

This document serves as the master blueprint for the exact sequence of events for both Sellers and Buyers on the Homemade CEO platform.

---

## Part 1: The Seller Journey (From Homemaker to CEO)

### Step 1: Frictionless Onboarding & Profile Creation
1. **Trigger:** A rural homemaker in Kerala sees a flyer or Meta Ad and sends a simple WhatsApp message (e.g., "Hi") to the Homemade CEO official number.
2. **Ghost Profile:** The backend server instantly recognizes a new phone number and silently spins up a secure database profile linked strictly to that number.
3. **Legal Compliance (DPDP Act 2023):** Jami's very first reply includes a standard data privacy consent prompt. The seller must reply "Agree" to allow the platform to store their personal data.
4. **Verification:** Once consented, Jami asks for their name and location to verify they reside within Kerala. 

### Step 2: The Educational Gate (LMS)
1. **Dashboard Login:** Jami provides a link to the Web Dashboard. The seller logs in using a secure WhatsApp OTP (One-Time Password) sent by Jami. No passwords are required.
2. **The Course:** The seller must complete a highly structured, Malayalam-language course on business fundamentals and photography. 
3. **Strict Rules:** The videos are non-skippable and cannot be fast-forwarded. 
4. **The Quiz:** Each module ends with a strict quiz. 
5. **Seamless Handoff:** The exact second the seller passes the final quiz, the backend triggers Jami. Jami proactively messages the seller on WhatsApp: *"Congratulations on completing the course! Let's upload your first product."*

### Step 3: Product Intake & Margin Validation
1. **Chat Intake:** The seller sends details and photos of their product (e.g., Mango Pickle) to Jami via WhatsApp.
2. **Photo Quality Review (Re-request Loop):** Before proceeding, Jami evaluates the photo. If the image is blurry, poorly lit, or cluttered, Jami gracefully rejects it and coaches the seller to retake it (e.g., *"That photo is a bit dark! Could you take another one near a window with sunlight?"*).
3. **Margin Calculator (The Hard Gate):** Jami strictly audits the product. She asks for the cost of raw materials, packaging, and labor. 
4. **Mathematical Validation:** Jami computes the gross margin. If it is below 100%, she rejects the listing and coaches the seller to adjust pricing. Once the margin is ≥100%, the product is approved.

### Step 4: Silent Generation & Agency Review
1. **Auto-Generation:** With the validated data, the backend silently and automatically generates a dedicated, beautiful **Product Landing Page** for that specific seller, and templates an **Ad Poster**.
2. **Ops Approval:** The Marketing Agency sees the generated assets in their English Admin Dashboard and approves them to go live.

---

## Part 2: The Buyer Journey (From Ad to Purchase)

### Step 5: Marketing & Meta Ads
1. **Ad Launch:** The Marketing Ops team launches targeted Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) using public LSGD funds, utilizing the auto-generated posters.
2. **Discovery:** A buyer browsing Instagram sees the ad for the homemade Mango Pickle and clicks on it.

### Step 6: The Landing Page
1. **Building Trust:** The buyer is not sent directly to a WhatsApp chat yet. They are redirected to the auto-generated **Product Landing Page**.
2. **The Pitch:** Here, they see premium photos, ingredients, the "Backed by Kudumbashree" trust badge, and the personal story of the rural homemaker who made it.
3. **The Hook:** At the bottom of the page, there is a prominent button: **"Order via WhatsApp."**

### Step 7: WhatsApp Checkout & Payment
1. **Order Initiation:** The buyer clicks the button, which opens their WhatsApp and sends a pre-filled message to the platform (e.g., *"I want to buy 1 Mango Pickle."*).
2. **Payment Link:** The system asks for their address and instantly replies with a secure **UPI Payment Link** (via Razorpay or Cashfree).
3. **Transaction:** The buyer pays via GPay/PhonePe.

---

## Part 3: Financials & Auditing

### Step 8: Revenue Split & Immutable Ledger
1. **The Split:** Once payment is confirmed, the backend calculates the split: **80% to the seller, 20% to the platform.**
2. **The Audit Log:** Every rupee of public ad spend, and every sale generated, is permanently recorded in the **Immutable Spend Ledger** to ensure absolute transparency for government audits.
3. **Seller Dashboard Update:** The seller logs into their Web Dashboard and instantly sees their new revenue tracked in large, clear typography.
